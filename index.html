Gohan Adobo Recipe

Deliciousness in every bite!

Chicken Adobo is an authentic Filipino dish and is one of the mostly recognized Filipino foods. Several sources who are experts in Asian food history say that the Filipinos were already cooking adobo even before Spanish colonization. According to them, cooking with vinegar preserves the meat.

This method is also considered as one of the earliest food preservation practice.
